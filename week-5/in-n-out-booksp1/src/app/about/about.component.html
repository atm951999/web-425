Ludwig van Beethoven, born 1770 in Bonn, Germany. He was the oldest of three children of Johann and Maria Magdalena van Beethoven. It was soon realized he was a talented pianist and he gave his first performance at age 8. He was employed as a court musician and studied under both Hayden and Mozart. In 1792 he relocated to Vienna where he became known as a virtuoso pianist. He lived in Vienna until his death in 1827.
